VR Info

Info

If you weren't aware VR stands for Virtual Reality. It is a computer generated environment with objects and scenes that appear to be real and it almost feel real. It makes the person using it feel as if they are immersed in their surroundings. This type of environment is found with a VR headset or "helmet". Different Brands of VR headsets are Oculus Rift Valve and HTC's Vive, and Sony PlayStation VR. All of these brands run off of external computers or game consoles which means they can offer sophisticated motio tracking, high-resolution screens, andthe best graphics possible. The best rand of VR headsets is Oculus Quest 2. The new version boasts improved resolution, a lighter headset, and its $100 cheaper. VR training in the military involves using head mounted displays (HMD) and data gloves to enable military personnel to interact with objects inside of a virtual environment.
